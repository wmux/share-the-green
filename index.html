About Share the Green

Following the success of last year’s inaugural Share the Mic & the Money Now event, Share the Green will engage hundreds of women-owned businesses across 16 cities with the goal of advancing female supplier diversity in the corporate supply chain. Selected women-owned businesses will pitch themselves to corporations across the U.S. Our aim is to advance female supplier diversity in the corporate supply chain. This event will encourage corporations to establish firm spending commitments to expand female voices and revenue in the communities served.
